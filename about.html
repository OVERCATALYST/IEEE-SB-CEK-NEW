Welcome to Our Community

IEEE Student Branch College of Engineering Karunagappally (STB 03951) is one of the oldest IEEE student branches in Kerala section, 4th branch in Travancore hub. And It is the second IEEE Student Branch in Kollam (Quilon) district. The group came into function on May 2010, in the early history of the institution. Student members of CEK take active participation in the IEEE meetings and workshops, earning many prizes and awards. The group started with Women in Engineering affinity group and now has 5 technical societies.

Our Mission

To provide students at CEK with opportunities for technical growth, professional development, and networking, empowering them to become future leaders in technology and engineering.

Our Vision

To be the most dynamic and influential student organization at the College of Engineering, Karunagappally, recognized for our commitment to technical excellence, innovation, and service to the community.

Our Journey Through Time

A timeline of milestones that have shaped the IEEE Student Branch at CEK.

2000: The Beginning

The IEEE Student Branch was officially inaugurated at CEK, founded by a passionate group of students and faculty with a vision to bring a global technical community to our campus.

2005: First Major Event

Hosted 'Innovate 2005', our first inter-collegiate technical symposium. The event was a massive success, establishing our presence within the IEEE Kerala Section.

2012: Chapter Formation

Launched our first technical sub-chapter, the IEEE Computer Society (CS), to cater to the growing interest in software development, data science, and competitive programming.

2020: Adapting & Growing

In response to global challenges, we successfully transitioned to a virtual model, hosting dozens of webinars and online workshops that expanded our reach far beyond the campus walls.

Present Day: A Vibrant Community

Today, IEEE SB CEK is a thriving community with hundreds of active members, multiple technical societies, and a proud legacy of award-winning projects, events, and volunteers.
