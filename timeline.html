2009

IEEE SB CEK was founded

After the approval from IEEE MGA board, IEEE SB CEK was established. This momentous achievement marked the official birth of our IEEE Student Branch, symbolizing our commitment to technology and innovation. With the hard work and dedication of our members, we embarked on this exciting journey, paving the way for endless opportunities and growth.

2008

Petition send for starting a SB

After gaining strong support from our college community, a formal application was submitted to IEEE Headquarters to establish a Student Branch. This application represented our collective commitment to promoting technology and collaboration.

2000

IEEE activities started

IEEE activities started at the college, paving the way for technological progress. Students came together to launch this initiative, promoting innovation and collaboration. The IEEE chapter's inception promises exciting opportunities for growth and learning.
