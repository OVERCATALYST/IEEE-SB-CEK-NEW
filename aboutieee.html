About IEEE

The world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

Our Mission

IEEE's core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.

Our Vision

IEEE will be essential to the global technical community and to technical professionals everywhere, and be universally recognized for the contributions of technology and of technical professionals in improving global conditions.

A Legacy of Innovation

From the dawn of electricity to the age of artificial intelligence, IEEE and its predecessor organizations have been at the forefront of technological advancement.

1884: The AIEE is Born

The American Institute of Electrical Engineers (AIEE) was founded in New York City. Its members were pioneers in the new field of electrical engineering, including icons like Thomas Edison and Alexander Graham Bell.

1912: The IRE is Formed

As radio technology emerged, the Institute of Radio Engineers (IRE) was established. The IRE was modeled on the AIEE but was devoted to the science and engineering of radio and electronics.

1963: A Historic Merger

On January 1, 1963, the AIEE and the IRE merged to form the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). This unification created a single organization that encompassed the full spectrum of electrical and electronics engineering.

Today: A Global Leader

With more than 420,000 members in over 160 countries, IEEE is the world's leading professional association for advancing technology. It is a trusted voice for engineering, computing, and technology information around the globe.

A Global Community

IEEE's organizational structure connects members locally, regionally, and internationally.

IEEE Regions

Globally, IEEE is divided into 10 geographic Regions. Each region serves as a hub for activities and networking. Our Student Branch is located in Region 10 (Asia-Pacific), the largest region by number of members.

Sections and Branches

Within each Region, members are organized into local Sections. Our Student Branch operates under the IEEE Kerala Section, one of the most vibrant sections in the world. Sections host local events and workshops. Below sections are the Student Branches, like ours, which form the grassroots of the IEEE community at universities and colleges.
